**How to use scrcpy to mirror your phone onto a Windows device**
-----------------------------------------------------------------

1. Using this path, open the following file: (../scrcpy-win64-v3.3.4/open_a_terminal_here.bat)
2. Enter the command: scrcpy --max-fps 45 --video-bit-rate 4M --render-driver opengl
*This is the recommended settings, but feel free to adjust them based on your computer's performance.*
